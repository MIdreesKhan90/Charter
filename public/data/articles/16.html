Discover the Benefits of Working with a Boutique Broker for Your Next Private Jet Rental.

For many it is well established that going through an agency rather than booking a private jet with an operator has many benefits. Yet something that only few understand is how exclusive a boutique charter broker can really be.

Even so, if you're looking to book a private jet for a luxurious vacation, booking with one of the big players in the private charter industry can be tempting. However, there are a number of undeniable advantages to working with a small broker company instead. In this article, we'll reveal all the reasons a small broker company can be a better choice when it comes to booking private jet charters.




For you and only you

One of the major advantages of working with a small broker company is the personalized touch. Small brokers are often run by a tight-knit team who are passionate about their work and mastered their field. They will surely go the extra mile to ensure that your private jet experience is the best and without a single hustle.

Unlike larger companies, where you might be passed from one department to another, with a small broker company you'll be working with the same people from start to finish, who will get to know your preferences and requirements. With boutique companies you are truly a VIP and will be treated as such.


Cost-Effective

While it might seem counterintuitive, working with a small broker company can actually help make the most out of your money. Large charter companies have huge overheads, including expensive marketing campaigns and corporate offices in prime locations. Small brokers, on the other hand, invest their efforts on finding the most exclusive solutions and are able to pass those on to their clients.

By working with a small broker, you may be able to get a better, unique vacation like no other and on your private jet charter that will cost you about the same as a pre-planned trip in a larger company.



By your dynamic schedule

Small broker companies are often more flexible than larger companies when it comes to booking private jets. Because they are not obligated to specific operators they are able to work with a wide range of aircraft. Meaning they can find the perfect aircraft for your trip, even if it's not in their own regular fleet. In addition, small brokers are often more willing to accommodate last-minute requests or changes to your itinerary.

Most large companies often ask for changing fees and are less cooperative when it comes to last minute changes. This level of flexibility can be invaluable, especially if you need to book a private jet at short notice, or you have a dynamic schedule that needs special attention.
